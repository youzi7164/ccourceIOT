Description

The purpose of this assignment is to make use of the data generated by the mobile phone sensor, through the analysis of the data, a visual display.This assignment is based on the behavioral data of Yue Xu in different time periods.Firstly, collect the data of different behaviors and activities, and compare the differences of data generated by different behaviors and different time.For example, acceleration is used to reflect walking frequency.In the process of collecting data, the mobile phone is tied to the ankle to ensure the accuracy of the data,There is also distance data that analyzes the distance between the face and the phone, indicating that the person using the phone is answering the phone.Gyroscope, the direction data can reflect the user's trajectory direction.Gravity data can indicate whether the phone is landscape or portrait, and if it is landscape, the user is more likely to play a game or watch a video.Based on the above analysis, the data characteristics of different behavior states are summarized.
